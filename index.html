League of Legends

League of legends is a MOBA (Mulitplayer Online Battle Arena) devolped by Riot Games. The game will start with 5 players on each side in a designated role and the goal is to destroy the other teams base. Top, jungle, Mid, ADC and Support

Generating Gold

Generating gold is a key part to the games mechanics, gold allows the player to purchase items to provide extra benefis for themselves and team

Roles & Lanes

Each Lanes in the Rift provides the team with different positions to play while in a match. Each Lane includes different Roles and Positions that the players may choose from to allow for each players playstyle.

Top Lane is at the top of the Rift ad is usually filled with roles such as tanks and bruisers. The Mid Lane is in the center of the Rift and is often occupied by mages and assassins. The Bottom Lane is at the bottom of the Rift and typically features a duo of an Attack Damage Carry (ADC) and a Support. The Jungle is the area between the lanes, where players known as Junglers roam to secure objectives and assist their teammates.

Neutral Objectives

In League of Legends, neutral objectives are powerful monsters on the map that neither team owns, and killing them gives team-wide advantages like permanent bonuses or stronger attacks. Teams fight over these objectives because controlling them makes it easier to win battles, take territory, and eventually win the game.

  • Dragon: Gives permanent, team-wide stat bonuses, with a powerful Dragon Soul after four kills.
  • Rift Herald: Helps destroy enemy towers by charging into them, giving early map control and gold.
  • Baron Nashor: Grants a strong temporary buff that empowers minions and helps teams push and end the game.
  • Elder Dragon: Provides an extremely strong combat buff that quickly finishes enemies and often decides the game.
